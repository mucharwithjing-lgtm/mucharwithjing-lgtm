## About Me

I am Zhao Tianhuan, an undergraduate student in the Department of German Studies at Beijing Institute of Technology (Class of 2024). My research interests span literature, mathematics, and cognitive science — modeling epic narratives, decoding emotion through EEG, and tracing sentiment through algorithms.

My favorite philosopher is **Hannah Arendt**. My favorite poet is **Louise Glück**.

In my spare time, I study religious studies, Greek mythology, and modern American and Latin American poetry and fiction (though my primary field is German literature). I practice calligraphy. Musically, I listen to Lana Del Rey and French chanson.

Fandom enthusiast. KalHo only.
